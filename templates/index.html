The Impact Of NBA Stats On Wins And Losses

We are going to predict the nba games’ results using logistic regression model and LSTM model to predict a team would win or losses the next match.

Why NBA Basketball?

Of all the machine learning project in the world we focused on the nba basketball. We picked this sport because of two reasons:
Firstly, there is a ton of data freely available.
Secondly, there are usually over a dozen games per day (which means plenty to bet on= money). Also we want to be able to go out to casino’s without losing money and Basketball is the game!
